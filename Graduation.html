Graduation at SUNY New Paltz

These graduation shots are more than just a cap and gown moment — they mark the end of one chapter and the quiet start of a bigger one. We shot portraits to capture the pride and growth up close, and wider landscape frames to show the journey — how far I’ve come, and how much lies ahead. Every detail in the photos reflects the grind: early mornings, late nights, self-doubt, breakthroughs. Whether it was walking campus halls or building projects that kept me up at 3 AM, it all led here. The contrast of sharp focus and open background reminds me to stay grounded while thinking big. This isn’t just a celebration — it’s proof that I’m just getting started.

Fredrick Armstrong
